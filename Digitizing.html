Digitizing And E-Archiving

Paperless” is the new efficient and productive approach to deal with your documents, providing more secure and cost effective way to store and retrieve your documents reducing the damage and loss ratio.
InfoGlobe participated in countless number of projects converting files and storing them in an electronic archive facilitate accessibility of filesApplying security standards and make, the documents content searchable using its Meta data.
Increasing efficiency, productivity, and documents are no longer restricted to the geographical location.
At InfoGlobe, we use the latest technology combined with years’ experience to turn your documents into digital files, which can be integrated into any existing IT system including our custom build Archiving System Al Hafiz E-Archive. Our knowledgeable and experienced staff have the capacity to convert large quantities of paper documents in a wide range of formats.

Our Service Includes

  • Preparing documents for scanning (removing staples, unfolding corners, etc.)
  • Files prepared to customer specifications in black and white, grey-scale or color, or scanned using a mix depending on the contents of particular pages
  • Comprehensive range of options for format of digital files
  • Data loading to e-Archiving
