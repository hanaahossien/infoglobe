About InfoGlobe

More than 15 years ago and with years of experience and a dream to provide IT services and solutions instead of products to create a new and reliable path to our clients in 2004 we decided to build InfoGlobe to develop IT sector in middle east.

Over the years, InfoGlobe provided solutions in different fields, covering Document management and Backlog Scanning combined with e-Archiving Solutions and software, build multiple software applications and solutions, working in Graphic, Web and Motion Design, providing Training sessions and Consulting for multiple clients inside and outside Egypt.

Vision

We aim to contribute to the technological development in the Middle East by precise, objective and professional standards according to Global quality standards.

Our Goals

  • To spread cultural awareness through the development of information and technology and to prepare the society to accept the transition to digital.
  • Managing change processes for digital transformation in a scientific and practical way.
  • Assist in the combination of technical development and human development so that it can cope with continuous change.
